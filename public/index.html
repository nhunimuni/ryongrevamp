About Us

Ryong. fire and herbs is a vegetarian restaurant with lots of vegan options. The team of Ryong has analyzed, refined and combined recipes from the Vietnamese and Japanese cuisines to an experimental and sophisticated streetfood with fresh spelt and pho noodles, soft and crunchy bao-burgers with tempura crusts and bento boxes with a fine selection of vegetables, pickles and herbs. Pretty much everything is homemade, from the spelt noodles and bao buns to mayonnaises, sauces, the vegan condesed milk and lemonades. The interior was designed by owner Tran Mai Huy Thong and his team at Oukan lab.
